Introduction

This project aims to visualise a certain dataset published by National Crime Records Bureau(NCRB) on either Crime, Suicides or Prison Statistics. I chose to work on a dataset about the Suicides in India by state and cause. I made a hypothesis to start of with and then I used the Tableau public tool to visualise the data and analyse it further to verify my hypothesis.

About the Dataset

The chosen dataset is obtained from Data World and it is called "Suicides in India". This was because of the inavailability to download from the NCRB website which is under maintenance. This provides data of suicides reported from 29 states, 7 UTs, ranging from 2001 - 2014 based on type, gender and age group of the concerned.

HYPOTHESIS: Is there a relationship between suicides committed with respect to the person’s gender, geographical location and their respective socio-economic factors?
